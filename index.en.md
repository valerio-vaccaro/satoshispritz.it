# Satoshi Spritz

Satoshi Spritz events are public meetups dedicated to Bitcoin, held regularly in different cities. They exist to build local communities of bitcoiners in Italy and abroad: people meet, have a drink together and talk about Bitcoin, without sponsors, financial product sales or <s>altcoin</s> <span class="bitcoin-orange">shitcoin</span> promotion.

The groups listed on this page follow, or are expected to follow, the shared Satoshi Spritz rules.

## ₿ What Is a Satoshi Spritz

A Satoshi Spritz is:

- a public event, open to everyone;
- a meetup where people can also take part anonymously;
- a Bitcoin-only space where only Bitcoin is discussed;
- a moment for education, conversation and mutual support among people interested in Bitcoin;
- a free-format event, with presentations encouraged when topics need deeper explanation.

Bitcoin's price and financial speculation are not central topics at Satoshi Spritz events. Trading, quick-profit talk and price predictions are considered off topic.

## 🚫 What a Satoshi Spritz Is Not

A Satoshi Spritz is not:

- an event for discussing, sponsoring or promoting <s>altcoins</s> <span class="bitcoin-orange">shitcoin</span>, tokens or scamcoins;
- a place to promote custodial solutions, custodial protocols or similar services;
- a channel for proposing investments, investment platforms or returns;
- a meetup about price, trading or financial speculation;
- an advertising space for channels, projects or events unrelated to Satoshi Spritz groups.

## 🕶️ Privacy

To protect everyone's privacy, sharing photos, videos or audio recordings of events and participants is always forbidden, both during and after the meetups.

## ⚠️ Security and Scams

As the community grows, it can attract malicious actors. To protect your funds and your digital identity, pay close attention to fake profiles, suspicious private messages and promises of profit.

### 👤 Cloned Profiles and Fake Channels

On Telegram it is easy to clone an account by copying the profile picture, name, bio and public information of admins or community members. Sometimes only one character in the username is changed, for example an `l` replaced by a `1`.

No Satoshi Spritz admin will ever contact you first in private chat to offer services, unsolicited support, investments or financial opportunities. If you receive a suspicious private message from a supposed admin, block and report the profile.

### 💬 Private Requests and Social Engineering

Be cautious of anyone, even if they claim to be an admin or a long-time community member, who contacts you privately to:

- ask for loans in euros, bitcoin or any other form of money;
- describe personal emergencies with promises of immediate repayment;
- ask for personal, financial or patrimonial information;
- ask how much you own, where you keep your funds or which wallets you use.

No member of the Satoshi Spritz staff will ever investigate your wealth, your privacy or how you custody your funds. No staff member will ever ask you for financial help or private loans.

### 🔐 Keys, Wallets and Funds

Never share your seed phrase, the 12 or 24 words of your wallet. Anyone asking for it, for any reason, is trying to steal your funds.

No Satoshi Spritz admin will ever ask you to send bitcoin, crypto or other funds for tests, shared investments, alleged technical checks, fundraisers or private initiatives.

### 📈 Profit Promises and Scam Platforms

Do not make investments through Telegram and do not register through suspicious links or platforms promising guaranteed returns, passive income or quick profits.

In Bitcoin, if something sounds too good to be true, it is probably a scam. The higher and faster the promised profit, the more likely it is to be a Ponzi scheme, a rug pull or another scam.

### ❓ When in Doubt

The spirit of Satoshi Spritz is educational, social and community-oriented. The meetups exist to talk about Bitcoin as a technology, protocol and monetary revolution, without promises of short-term wealth.

Satoshi Spritz groups do not provide financial advice, sell courses or promote investment packages. Study and understanding are the only sustainable path: knowledge itself is the first real wealth.

Always verify sources and, before taking any doubtful action, ask publicly in the official group.

## 🤝 Buying, Selling and Trading

Satoshi Spritz groups are communities for education and discussion, not buying or selling channels.

- Buying, selling and trading are forbidden during events.
- Satoshi Spritz groups must not be listed on exchange platforms, to prevent channels from being used for purposes unrelated to education and community life.

## 💸 Fundraising

Satoshi Spritz events are non-profit. They do not organize fundraisers, ask for fees or solicit donations.

Requests for funds for events organized, hosted or endorsed by one or more Satoshi Spritz groups are expressly forbidden.

## 🌐 Satoshi Spritz Connect

Satoshi Spritz Connect is the virtual Satoshi Spritz held in its dedicated Telegram channel.

It lets people hear voices from different groups remotely, without leaving their city. It can also help smaller local groups: people who follow the virtual meetup can bring the same topics back to in-person events.

Satoshi Spritz Connect follows the same rules as Satoshi Spritz and Officine Bitcoin.

## 🛠️ Officine Bitcoin

Officine Bitcoin organizes free recurring online events about Bitcoin and related topics.

Each meetup lasts up to 30 minutes: the first part is dedicated to the lesson, while the remaining time is for questions and participant support.

Lessons take place on Telegram in the [Officine Bitcoin](https://officinebitcoin.it/) group.
